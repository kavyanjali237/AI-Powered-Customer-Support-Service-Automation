# Stakeholder Mapping

## Project Sponsor
- Defines business objectives.
- Approves deliverables.

## Salesforce System Administrator
- Configures Salesforce.
- Implements Prompt Builder.
- Enables Agentforce.
- Manages security.

## Inspection Team
- Primary end users.
- Creates inspection records.
- Uses AI-generated summaries.

## Support Team
- Reviews inspection information.
- Assists customers.

## Management
- Monitors inspections.
- Makes business decisions.

## IT Support
- Maintains the system.
- Resolves technical issues.
